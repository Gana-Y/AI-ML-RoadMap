## 💡 Bonus Tips to Get Hired Faster

**1. Target Startups First**
Big tech won't hire fresh AI Engineers. Target Series A/B startups,
health-tech and ed-tech companies — they hire faster and give more 
responsibility.

**2. Apply for Remote and Hybrid Roles**
Bengaluru has 1000+ AI startups. Also apply to Hyderabad, Pune 
and Chennai. Mention "Open to Remote/Hybrid" on your LinkedIn.

**3. Participate in Hackathons**
Register for SIH, HackWithInfy, Unstop hackathons. Even participation 
gives you a great story for interviews.

**4. Build a Kaggle Profile**
Complete Kaggle Learn courses and join competitions. Even a bronze 
medal can replace 3 months of explanation in an interview.

**5. The DM Strategy**
Every week DM 10 AI Engineers at companies you want to join.
Ask for 15 minutes to learn what they look for in interns.
30% will reply and 10% will refer you.

**6. The Learn → Build → Post Loop**
This is the most powerful habit you can build:
1. Learn something new (1 hr)
2. Build something with it (1-2 hrs)  
3. Post about it on LinkedIn with GitHub link

Repeat 3x per week for 8 months 🔥

**7. Consistency Over Intensity**
1-2 hours daily for 8 months = 480 hours of focused learning.
More than enough to become hireable. Show up every single day.
